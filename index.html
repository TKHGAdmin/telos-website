FAQ

The Lifestyle system is built for people who are busy. Training is 3–4 sessions per week, 45–60 minutes each. Your program is designed around your actual schedule - not an ideal one. Travel weeks, sick kids, deadlines - the plan adjusts. The point isn't perfection. It's protecting your floor so momentum never dies.

Knowing how to train and executing consistently are two different problems. You don't need education - you need external structure. A coach who tracks your execution, enforces your non-negotiables, and adjusts the plan when life gets in the way. That's the infrastructure competitive sport used to provide. Telos reinstalls it.

Growth gives you custom programming and async text support. Lifestyle gives you the full system - weekly 1:1 calls with Thomas, custom nutrition, daily execution scoring, direct text access, and busy-week adjustments. Growth is a program. Lifestyle is the complete operating system. If you're serious about rebuilding the structure that used to make execution automatic, Lifestyle is the tier.
