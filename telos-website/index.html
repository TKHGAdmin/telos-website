About Thomas

I Know What Structure
Looks Like at the Top.

I dropped out of college and spent three years trialing with six professional soccer clubs while playing semi-professionally. I know what competitive athletic structure looks like at the highest level — and what happens when it disappears.

Later, I built a personal training business while running a tech startup that eventually failed. That failure clarified one thing: execution — not ideas, not motivation — is the only thing that compounds.

Telos Fitness exists because I had to reverse-engineer discipline from scratch. Now I install that system for men who once had it — and lost the structure that made it automatic.
